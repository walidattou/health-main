Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Ritual vitamins.

When should I take my Ritual vitamins?

Ritual recommends taking your vitamins at the same time each day to establish a routine. You can take them with or without food. Many customers prefer to take them in the morning or before bed. Ritual multivitamins are designed with delayed-release technology that helps reduce nausea, even on an empty stomach.

Why are Ritual capsules so different?

Ritual capsules use patented "beadlet-in-oil" technology that allows combining water-soluble and fat-soluble ingredients in one capsule, while maintaining their stability and improving absorption. The transparent design lets you see exactly what you're taking. Added peppermint oil helps keep your breath fresh and mask any unpleasant aftertaste.

How long does it take to see results?

Vitamins work best when taken regularly over an extended period. Some customers report improvements in energy and vitality after a few weeks, but for more significant changes (such as improved nail quality, hair, or nutritional levels), it is recommended to take the vitamins for at least 3-4 months. Ritual suggests getting a blood test before starting and after a few months to objectively measure improvements.

Are Ritual products vegan?

Yes, all Ritual products are formulated with 100% vegan ingredients. Omega-3 DHA comes from microalgae rather than fish, and vitamin D3 is extracted from lichen rather than sheep lanolin. Capsules are made from plant-based algae rather than animal gelatin. This approach not only avoids animal products but also reduces potential contaminants like heavy metals often found in fish-based supplements.

How can I cancel or modify my subscription?

You can manage all aspects of your subscription directly from your online account. To modify, pause, or cancel, log in to your account, go to the "Subscriptions" section, and follow the instructions. You can also contact customer service via email or chat. There are no cancellation fees and no minimum commitment. To ensure changes are applied to the current cycle, make them at least 48 hours before your next billing date.

Can I take Ritual with other supplements?

Ritual multivitamins are designed to provide essential nutrients without the risk of overdose, even in combination with a balanced diet. However, if you are taking other specific supplements or medications, it is recommended to consult your doctor to avoid any potential interactions or excessive intake of certain nutrients. Ritual has deliberately excluded certain nutrients that most people get enough of in their diet, such as vitamin C, to avoid overdose risks.
