Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Snow and our teeth whitening products.

Is Snow safe for sensitive teeth?

Yes, Snow has been specifically formulated to be safe and effective even for people with sensitive teeth. Our serum contains desensitizing ingredients that protect dental nerves while whitening effectively. Unlike other whitening systems, Snow operates at low temperatures and does not dry out teeth, which significantly reduces the risk of sensitivity.

If you have particularly sensitive teeth, we recommend starting with short sessions (5-10 minutes) and gradually increasing the duration according to your comfort.

How long does it take to see results?

Most users start to see visible results after 3 to 5 days of regular use. Full results are typically achieved after 21 days of daily use. The extent of results depends on several factors:

  • The nature and intensity of dental stains
  • Consistency in product use
  • Duration of whitening sessions
  • Your natural tooth shade

To maximize and maintain results, we recommend using the product daily for 21 days, then 1-2 times per week for maintenance.

How does Snow work on dental stains?

Snow uses a dual-action whitening technology:

  1. Active Serum: Our formula contains oxidizing agents that penetrate dental enamel to break down stain molecules into smaller, less visible components.
  2. LED Technology: Our LED light device emits specific wavelengths that activate and accelerate the serum's reaction, amplifying its effectiveness.

This system works on most types of stains, including those caused by coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco, and certain pigmented foods. It is worth noting that Snow is more effective on extrinsic stains (surface) than intrinsic stains (internal) or discolorations due to medical causes.

Is Snow safe for dental enamel?

Yes, when used as directed, Snow is safe for dental enamel. Our formula has been developed to be effective while remaining gentle on teeth. Unlike some aggressive whitening systems, Snow:

  • Uses whitening agents in safe concentrations
  • Maintains a balanced pH to protect enamel
  • Includes remineralizing ingredients that strengthen enamel
  • Avoids harsh abrasives that could erode the tooth surface

Independent studies have confirmed that our system, when used according to instructions, does not damage dental enamel or gums.

How do I use the Snow app with my kit?

The Snow app is available for free on iOS and Android and connects to your wireless whitening device via Bluetooth. To use it:

  1. Download the Snow app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Create an account or log in
  3. Enable Bluetooth on your phone
  4. Follow the instructions to connect your device
  5. Use the app to control light intensity, track session duration, and receive reminders

The app also allows you to track your progress with before/after photos, access personalized tips, and set reminders for your treatments. Note that the app is compatible only with Snow's wireless kits and not with the standard wired version.

What is the lifespan of Snow products?

The lifespan of Snow products varies depending on the type:

  • LED Device: The LED device has a lifespan of approximately 50,000 hours of use, which equates to several years of normal use.
  • Whitening Serums: Unopened serum pens can be stored for up to 2 years. Once opened, they remain effective for about 6 months.
  • Complete Kit: A standard Snow kit contains enough serum for approximately 75 treatments, which corresponds to about 1 year of regular use (initial treatment + maintenance).

To maximize the lifespan of your products, store serums at room temperature in a dry place, reseal tightly after each use, and clean the LED device according to instructions.
