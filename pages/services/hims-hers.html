Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Hims & Hers.

Questions about Hims
Questions about Hers
Are Hims medications legitimate and safe?

Yes, all medications available through Hims are approved by health authorities and are identical to those you would receive in a traditional pharmacy. Each treatment is prescribed by a certified doctor after evaluating your medical situation. Medications are provided by licensed pharmacies that adhere to the same strict standards as any other pharmacy.

How does the online medical consultation work?

The process begins with a detailed medical questionnaire that collects information about your health, symptoms, and medical history. This information is reviewed by a certified doctor who evaluates whether treatment is appropriate for you. In some cases, the doctor may request additional information or a video consultation. If treatment is prescribed, the prescription is sent to a partner pharmacy that prepares and ships your treatment.

Can I use my health insurance with Hims?

Currently, Hims primarily operates on a direct payment model without involving insurance, which helps keep prices low and transparent. However, some insurers may partially reimburse medications purchased through Hims. In such cases, Hims can provide a detailed invoice that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. We recommend contacting your insurer directly to understand your specific coverage.

How do Hims subscriptions work?

Hims subscriptions are flexible and designed for your convenience. After your first order, you will automatically receive refills at regular intervals (usually monthly or quarterly depending on the product). You can easily manage your subscription online, changing the frequency, pausing, or canceling at any time without penalty. Reminders are sent before each shipment, giving you the opportunity to adjust your order if necessary.

How long does it take to see results with Hims treatments?

The time to effectiveness varies depending on the treatment. Medications for erectile dysfunction typically work within 30-60 minutes and are taken as needed. Hair loss treatments usually require 3-4 months of regular use before visible results are observed, and effects continue to improve with ongoing use. Mental health treatments may take 4-6 weeks to reach their full effectiveness. Your Hims doctor will provide specific expectations for your particular treatment.

How can I get a birth control prescription through Hers?

To obtain birth control through Hers, you start by completing an online medical questionnaire detailing your health history, contraceptive preferences, and any relevant medical conditions. A certified doctor reviews this information and, if appropriate, prescribes the contraception that best suits your situation. In some cases, additional information or a video consultation may be required. Once approved, your contraception is discreetly shipped to your home, usually with automatic renewals to avoid interruptions.

Are Hers skincare products suitable for all skin types?

Hers offers a range of skincare products formulated for different skin types and specific concerns. During your consultation, you will be guided to choose the products most suitable for your skin type (normal, dry, oily, combination, or sensitive) and your specific issues such as acne, hyperpigmentation, or aging. If you have a complex dermatological condition or known allergies, the doctor may recommend specific formulations or advise you to consult a dermatologist in person for a more thorough evaluation.

How does Hers' mental health service work?

Hers' mental health service includes two main components: therapy and medication treatments. For therapy, you are connected with a licensed therapist for regular online sessions. For medication treatments for anxiety or depression, you complete an online assessment that is reviewed by a psychiatrist. If treatment is recommended, it is prescribed and delivered regularly. Follow-up is provided to adjust the treatment if necessary. Both options can be used separately or complementarily depending on your needs.

Can Hers subscriptions be canceled at any time?

Yes, Hers subscriptions are flexible and can be modified or canceled at any time without cancellation fees. You can manage your subscription directly from your online account, where you can change delivery frequency, pause temporarily, or cancel completely. To ensure changes are applied, it is recommended to make them at least 48 hours before the scheduled processing date of your next order, which is usually indicated in your account.

Do Hers hair treatments really work?

Hers hair treatments contain clinically proven ingredients like minoxidil, which is approved to stimulate hair regrowth in women. Effectiveness may vary among individuals and depends on several factors, including the type of hair loss, the stage of loss, and consistency in using the treatment. Clinical studies show that the majority of regular users experience reduced shedding and/or improved hair density after 3-6 months of continuous use. For optimal results, it is important to follow instructions and use the product daily over the long term.
