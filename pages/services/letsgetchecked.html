Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions about LetsGetChecked.

Are LetsGetChecked tests reliable?

Yes, all LetsGetChecked tests are clinically validated and analyzed in certified laboratories according to the highest standards. The analysis methods are the same as those used in hospitals and clinics. To ensure accuracy, each test includes detailed instructions on the optimal time to collect the sample and factors that may affect the results.

How is the sample collected?

The collection method depends on the type of test. For blood tests, the kit includes a finger-prick device with step-by-step instructions. For STI tests, depending on the chosen test, it may involve a urine sample, vaginal swab, or blood sample. All kits contain detailed instructions, and assistance is available by phone or chat if you have questions during the process.

Is the service truly confidential?

LetsGetChecked guarantees complete confidentiality. Kits are delivered in discreet packaging without any mention of their contents. Your results are protected by encryption and are only accessible to you and LetsGetChecked's medical team. No information is shared with third parties, including your primary care physician, without your explicit consent.

What happens if my results are abnormal?

If your results are abnormal, a member of LetsGetChecked's medical team will contact you by phone to discuss your results in detail, answer your questions, and advise you on next steps. For certain conditions (such as treatable STIs), they can even prescribe treatment and ship it directly to your home if necessary. You will also receive a report that you can share with your primary care physician.

Are LetsGetChecked tests covered by health insurance?

Generally, LetsGetChecked tests are not directly reimbursed by standard health insurance plans or social security. However, some private insurance providers may partially cover these tests under certain conditions. It is recommended to contact your insurance provider directly to verify your coverage. LetsGetChecked can provide a detailed invoice to facilitate your reimbursement request.

How long does it take to receive results?

Once your sample is received by the laboratory, results are typically available within 2 to 5 business days, depending on the type of test. You will receive an email notification when your results are ready to be viewed on your secure online account. For positive or abnormal results requiring immediate attention, you will be contacted directly by the medical team, usually within 24 hours of obtaining the results.
