Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Quip.

Is the Quip toothbrush as effective as other electric toothbrushes?

Yes, Quip uses sonic vibrations at 15,000 movements per minute that effectively clean teeth and gums. While some premium brushes offer higher speeds and additional modes, Quip was designed with dentists' input to provide an optimal brushing experience. Many dentists emphasize that technique and brushing duration are more important than power, and Quip's 2-minute timer helps maintain adequate brushing time.

How do I replace the battery in my Quip toothbrush?

Replacing the battery is simple: twist the base of the toothbrush counterclockwise to open it, remove the old AAA battery, insert a new one, and screw the base back on. A battery typically lasts 3 months with normal use (two 2-minute brushings per day). To preserve battery life, ensure the toothbrush is completely dry after use and avoid leaving it in the "on" position when not in use.

Can I use Quip if I have sensitive teeth?

Yes, Quip is ideal for sensitive teeth. The brush uses premium nylon bristles with rounded tips that are gentle on gums and tooth enamel. The sonic vibrations are effective without being harsh, unlike some rotating brushes that can be too abrasive. Many users with sensitive gums or recession report improvement after switching to Quip. If you have specific concerns, consult your dentist for personalized recommendations.

How does the Quip subscription work?

The Quip subscription is designed to be flexible and commitment-free. After the initial purchase of your toothbrush, you are automatically enrolled to receive a new brush head every 3 months (unless you choose not to subscribe at checkout). You can easily add other products to your subscription, change delivery frequency, pause, or cancel anytime from your online account. Subscription fees are automatically charged before each shipment, and all deliveries come with free shipping.

Is the Quip toothbrush dentist-approved?

Yes, Quip was developed with the help of dentists and has received the American Dental Association (ADA) Seal of Acceptance, which attests to its safety and effectiveness. The brush was designed to encourage good brushing habits recommended by dental professionals: 2-minute brushing, regular replacement of the brush head every 3 months, and adequate coverage of all areas of the mouth with the 30-second quadrant timer. Many dentists recommend Quip to their patients, especially for its convenient brush head replacement reminder system.

What are the international shipping options for Quip?

Quip currently ships to many countries, including France, with international shipping fees that vary by destination. International orders are typically delivered within 10 to 15 business days. Please note that international customers may be subject to customs duties or import taxes depending on local regulations. For international subscriptions, Quip generally offers quarterly shipments to optimize shipping costs. For specific details about your country, check the "International Shipping" section on Quip's official website.
