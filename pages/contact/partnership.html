Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses can advertise on HealthCompare?

We welcome healthcare providers, pharmacies, telehealth services, health product brands, medical device companies, and wellness services that align with our mission of improving health outcomes.

Our starter package begins at $299/month with no long-term commitment required. Custom packages are available for larger campaigns.

Once approved, most display ads can go live within 24-48 hours. Sponsored content typically requires 5-7 business days for creation and approval.
