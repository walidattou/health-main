Different Types of Electric Toothbrushes

Sonic Toothbrushes

Sonic toothbrushes use high-frequency vibrations to clean teeth:

  • Vibrations up to 31,000 movements per minute
  • Deep cleaning without aggressiveness
  • Ideal for sensitive gums

Oscillating-Rotating Toothbrushes

These toothbrushes combine rotation and pulsation:

  • Circular movements of the brush head
  • Effective against dental plaque
  • More pronounced mechanical action
